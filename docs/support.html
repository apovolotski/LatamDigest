Support

Need help with Latam Digest? Reach out for technical support, bug reports, or questions about the app.

Email alexander.povolotski@gmail.com
Response time Usually within 2 business days.
Support topics App issues, feature feedback, localization fixes, and content feed questions.

When contacting support

Please include as much detail as possible so the issue can be reproduced quickly:

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The app version you’re using.
  • What happened and what you expected instead.
  • A screenshot if the issue is visual.

Content questions

Latam Digest organizes headlines and source links from public news feeds. If you spot a broken link, outdated story, or low-quality source, send the country, category, and story title so it can be reviewed.
