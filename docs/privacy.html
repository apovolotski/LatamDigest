Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Latam Digest handles information when you use the app. Effective date: March 24, 2026.

Overview

Latam Digest is designed to be lightweight and privacy-conscious. The app does not require account creation and does not ask users to provide names, phone numbers, or email addresses inside the app.

Information stored on your device

The app stores limited preferences locally on your device so the experience works as expected.

  • Your selected language.
  • Your selected countries.
  • Your preferred daily notification time.

This information is stored using standard iOS local storage and is not used to identify you.

News content and network requests

Latam Digest retrieves public news digest data from static JSON files hosted through GitHub Pages / GitHub infrastructure and opens source news articles in Safari or an in-app browser view. When you open external articles, those third-party sites may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Latam Digest schedules local notifications on your device to remind you about your daily digest. Notification preferences are stored locally and are not used to build advertising profiles.

Analytics and tracking

Latam Digest does not currently use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technology.

Children’s privacy

Latam Digest is a general audience news app and is not directed to children under 13.
